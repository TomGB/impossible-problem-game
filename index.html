The aim of the game is to move the pieces from their 'home' position on the righthand side of the board onto the playing area,
the grid on the left, in such a way that where the side of a piece is next to the side of another piece, the two sides match.

A match is a long bar next to a short bar of the same colour.

The pieces can be rotated while they are on the right but not once they have been moved.

To rotate a piece, click in the top left corner of the piece.

To move a piece click on the centre of the piece then click in an empty square on the grid.

To remove a piece from the grid, click on it to return it to its home.
